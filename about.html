About Us

Revolutionizing Dance's Digital Space

Building bridges between traditional artistry and modern technology

KatikaNaMe was born from a passion for Kenyan dance and a recognition of the challenges dancers face in showcasing their talent professionally. We saw the need for a platform that understands the unique styles, cultural richness, and authentic stories of African dance.

With deep experience in both dance and technology within the Kenyan creative space, we've created a solution that offers cutting-edge digital presentation tools while honoring the heritage and artistry that makes our dance community special.

Whether you're a traditional dancer preserving cultural heritage, a contemporary artist pushing boundaries, or someone exploring the fusion of different styles, KatikaNaMe gives you the tools to present your work with the professionalism it deserves.

We believe that every dancer has a story worth telling and talent worth sharing. Our platform democratizes access to professional portfolio creation, eliminating barriers that have historically prevented talented artists from reaching wider audiences.

Meet The Founder

Passionate about dance, driven by technology, committed to community

Pauline Karega

Founder & Tech Lead

Freestyle dancer from Nairobi and PhD student specializing in health and environmental data science. Pauline believes in the power of open data for research and sees it as the key to revolutionizing performance art in Kenya.

Her unique combination of artistic passion and technical expertise drives KatikaNaMe's mission to bridge the gap between traditional dance and modern digital platforms. This platform represents step one in her vision to transform how African creatives showcase and monetize their talents.
